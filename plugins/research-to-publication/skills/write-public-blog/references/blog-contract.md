# Public blog contract

- Use Traditional Chinese and Chinese-first terminology.
- Explain one conceptual dependency at a time.
- Keep paragraphs short; introduce English only at first necessary use.
- Identify papers by author, year, and title at first substantive mention.
- Preserve uncertainty, counterexamples, and the difference between evidence and hypothesis.
- Match the target site's current typography and content width.
- Require explicit content approval before publication.
